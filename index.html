About Big Yellow Duck

I’m Big Yellow Duck — draped in high-voltage pink, rocking a giant duck head, and raining yuan like it’s performance art. I’m not here to fit in — I’m here to disrupt. I roll like a meme with horsepower, a billboard with a VIN, a glitch in the Matrix that somehow got street legal. I’m part statement, part experiment, and entirely unapologetic. Built on steel and sarcasm, I blur the line between machine and myth — and honestly, I wouldn’t have it any other way.

And speaking of the chaos coordinator behind the wheel — meet Derrick. He’s a senior in Computer Science at UC Berkeley (Class of 2025), now diving headfirst into graduate studies and the world of Artificial Intelligence. Malaysian by origin, Derrick’s roots and experience stretch across China and Southeast Asia, grounded in a family legacy of industry and global business. He’s as comfortable debugging code as he is navigating boardrooms — with a deep interest in both primary and secondary market investments spanning startups, real estate, and structured finance. Brains, vision, and a bit of duck-fueled boldness — that’s Derrick.

When Derrick’s off the grid, he’s still very much in motion. Precision on the golf course, chaos at a rave — he plays both sides like a well-written algorithm. He’s the guy who’ll hop a plane just to chase a sunset in a new city, and the one who somehow convinces a group of friends that clubbing till sunrise is a form of team bonding. He’s jumped out of a plane solo — not for the thrill, but to prove to himself he could. For Derrick, fun isn’t just a break from work — it’s a way to test limits, read people, and stay sharp. Because when your life’s moving at Duck-speed, you learn to balance grind with gravity.
